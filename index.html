chronology of Mr Soekarno's life


  • 1901 born in Surabaya
  • 1908 moved to mojokerto to pursue her education
  • 1919 completed his education
  • 1929 in jail for calling the Dutch imperialists
  • 1931 released from prison
  • 1931-1945 awakening the nationalist spirit of the people, fighting and continuing dialogue with the Dutch and Japanese
  • 1945 proclaimed Indonesia's independence for the first time
  • 1945 become president of indonesia
  • 1965 stepped down from the presidency by the g30spki movement
  • 1970 Soekarno died
